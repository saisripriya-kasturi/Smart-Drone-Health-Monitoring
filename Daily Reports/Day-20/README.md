# Day 20 – Research Document

## Project Title

**AI-Based Smart Agricultural Drone for Precision Farming**

## Introduction

* Studied the application of AI and drone technology in modern agriculture.
* Explored how drones with sensors can monitor crops and improve farming efficiency.
* Real-time aerial monitoring helps detect crop stress, diseases, and irrigation issues.

## Problem Statement

* Manual crop inspection is time-consuming and difficult for large farms.
* Late detection of diseases affects crop productivity.
* Excessive use of pesticides and fertilizers increases cost and environmental impact.

## Proposed Solution

* An AI-powered agricultural drone equipped with cameras and sensors monitors crop health.
* AI analysis identifies diseases, pests, and nutrient deficiencies.
* Precision spraying is performed only in affected areas.
* Crop data is stored and monitored through a cloud dashboard.

## Objectives

* Develop an intelligent drone system for automated crop monitoring.
* Reduce chemical usage through precision spraying.
* Provide real-time crop health information to farmers.

## Expected Outcomes

* Early disease detection and improved crop yield.
* Reduced resource wastage and operational costs.
* Support for sustainable and smart farming practices.

## Conclusion

* AI-based agricultural drones provide an efficient solution for precision farming.
* Integration of AI, IoT, and drone technology improves crop monitoring and productivity.
