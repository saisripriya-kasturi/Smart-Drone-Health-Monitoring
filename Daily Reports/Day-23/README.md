# Day 23 – Pricing Strategy & Government Policies

## Objective
Study pricing strategies for drone products and understand the government policies supporting the drone industry.

## Activities Performed
- Studied different pricing models used by drone manufacturers.
- Analyzed the importance of cost-based, value-based, competitive, and subscription pricing.
- Researched government initiatives including Drone Rules 2021, Digital Sky Platform, PLI Scheme, and Startup India.
- Identified the impact of pricing strategies and government regulations on business growth.

## Deliverables
- Pricing Strategy Report
- Government Policies Report

## Learning Outcomes
- Understood different business pricing models.
- Learned how government policies influence the drone industry.
- Gained knowledge of commercialization strategies for technology products.
- Improved understanding of entrepreneurship in the drone sector.
